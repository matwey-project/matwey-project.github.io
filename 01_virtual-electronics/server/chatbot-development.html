Automate repeated client tasks, increase outcome and reduce customer care costs by using a chatbot. Virtual Electronics is always ready to help you develop a virtual helper tailored according to your businesses’ needs and consistent with the best industry practices.

Smart chatbot for your business

1Chatbot for Banking & Finance

This chatbot is designed to provide personal financial information to your customers, such as outstanding balance, available credit limit, payment dates, banking services and products, history of operations, interest calculations, etc. It can be also used to send alerts to your customers and assist them in making payments and fund transfers.

2Traveler’s assistant Chatbot

This chatbot is designed to help travelers find and book the most suitable flights, train and bus rides, pay for the tickets and alert them about any schedule changes. It also helps with hotels, house and car rentals, event tickets, restaurant bookings and weather forecasts.

Read more or try a demo

3E-commerce Chatbot

Functionality of this Chatbot includes online navigation in stores and malls, finding development prospects, reporting a lost item to the shop administration, tracking delivery status and sending alerts. It also facilitates in-app purchases, promotes best deals and offers coupons. E-commerce Chatbot is a great way to increase sales and remarketing.

4Insurance Chatbot

Insurance Chatbot can be used by your customers for comparing plans, reminding insurance policies and assisting with filling and submitting insurance statements. Having a 24/7 Chatbot is particularly important in an industry with clients who may require immediate attention at any time of the day.

5Chatbot for medical services

This medical Chatbot is perfect for making doctor appointments, schedule changes, sending reminders, collecting patient data, etc. If necessary, the bot can offer a preliminary diagnosis and suggested treatment based on the symptoms. It will become a great helper to medical personnel, pharmacists and patients.

6HR Chatbot

Use this Chatbot to rationalize your HR’s work with the personnel. It helps consult on job opportunities, open vacancies, legal labor requirements, corporate policies and events. It can automatically conduct surveys among the employees and automate tasks related to hiring such as data collection, interview appointments, etc.

7Support service Chatbot

Automate the most common support tasks such as password restoration, account management, access to common office devices or web folders, FAQs etc.

8Corporate chatbot

You can entrust this Chatbot with day-to-day corporate needs such as scheduling the conference room, booking business trips, accessing corporate contact numbers, policies and guidelines, collecting feedback and suggestions from the employees, etc.
