Employment Opportunitues

Description

This is a high energy work place that requires our employees to be able to perform in a fast paced environment. You will have huge responsibilities and will be expected to bring a positive attitude to every shift. Also you will be responsible for every aspect involved in the coffee drive thru setting, such as exemplary customer service, taking orders, prepping and make drinks, and keeping the shops in a clean, orderly and tidy condition.

Responsibilities

Be Awesome, Be Positive and Be Proactive. We thrive and operate in a team setting. When writing your email to us, please include an instance or event where you work or played in a team setting?

Qualifications

NO prior barista experience is a plus. We need someone who can easily get to Ogden and Layton, (reliable transportation) can work a flexible schedule, and someone who truly "Promotes Positive Energy" each and every day.

Additional Information

We are looking for our next rock star. WE are looking for someone to join our team who has a flexible schedule, a love for coffee and a love for life. Please EMAIL us a paragraph telling us who you are, why you want to work for the Daily Rise Expresso and what you can "bring to the team."
