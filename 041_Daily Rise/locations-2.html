Daily Rise Expresso, Layton

Established in 2008, we wanted to bring the Positive Energy down to Davis County. Located on the popular corner of Antelope and Main, you’ll find our log cabin coffee shop drive thru with our Roast Facility located directly behind. We try to only use the best ingredients at Daily Rise and provide all natural products wherever possible–our syrups, coffee & tea, smoothies, and other ingredients are top of the line.

We really focus on quality and consistency between the two locations. In Layton, you will find the SAME baristas, SAME signature espresso and non-espresso drinks and SAME amazing customer service!
