Webinar: Transforming Energy Management - Trusts taking the lead on carbon reduction - 20 March 2021

Everybody wants to see action on climate change and improvements to the life chances of young people. The education sector is in a unique position to benefit from the energy savings and income-generating opportunities afforded by effective energy management strategies. In this webinar, Jonathan Coyles explores the opportunities and solutions available and share how some of the country’s largest trusts are leading the way.

The session:

Highlights the opportunities for financial savings and carbon reductions. Explores three key areas for action: 1. Procurement strategy 2. Energy demand reduction 3. Renewable energy projects. Explains key technical considerations and funding options.
