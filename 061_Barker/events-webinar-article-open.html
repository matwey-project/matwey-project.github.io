Webinar: Transforming Energy Management - Trusts taking the lead on carbon reduction - 20 March 2021

Everybody wants to see action on climate change and improvements to the life chances of young people. The education sector is in a unique position to benefit from the energy savings and income-generating opportunities afforded by effective energy management strategies. In this webinar, Jonathan Coyles explores the opportunities and solutions available and share how some of the countryâ€™s largest trusts are leading the way.
