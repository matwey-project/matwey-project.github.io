Market Field School-
Four Classroom Extension

  • Client: Market Field Learning Community
  • Value: £1.1m
  • Location: Elmstead Market
  • Sector: Education,
  • Services: Architecture & Design, Project Management, Quantity Surveying, Structural & Civil Engineering,

Market Field is a school for pupils with moderate learning difficulties (MLD). They offer specialist provision for pupils with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) at all Key Stages in 5-10 age range, OFSTED judged personal development of its pupils as outstanding.

Barker were appointed due to their specialist knowledge and education experience, to design and plan a four classroom extension to provide additional accommodation resulting from increased demand. As well as to facilitate the amalgamation of a split site to improve efficiency of service delivery.
