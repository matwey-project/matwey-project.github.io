Building surveyor

Structural & Civil Engineering

See more details
Collapse

The role

Due to our continued growth we currently have vacancy for a Building Surveyor, who will work on a range of projects and professional services in the education, residential and commercial sectors.

The position will be based at our Cambridge office and offers a good remuneration package. There is an excellent opportunity for progression and career development, and a variety of work to suit high calibre individuals.

Responsabilities

The role will suit a project-orientated Building Surveyor. Principal tasks will be:

  • Design & Specification writing
  • Tendering & Procurement
  • Contract Administration and Project Management
  • Feasibility Studies
  • CDM/Principal Designer

The Person

  • Degree in Building Surveying or equivalent.
  • MRICS Chartered Building Surveyors preferred but not essential (full APC support given).
  • If working towards APC, a minimum of 2+ years’
  • Experience of design, specification and contractadministration using JCT contracts.
  • Education experience an advantage.
  • This role will be subject to a DBS Enhanced check.
  • Location: Cambridge
  • Type: Full-time
