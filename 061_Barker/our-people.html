Our people

Barker is a RIBA and RICS Chartered Practice employing an experienced team of chartered architects, surveyors engineers and project managers. Our team work collaboratively to analyse projects in terms of design, functionality, cost and programme in order to ensure that the most appropriate and balanced solutions are delivered.

Our key strength is our staff and the depth and diversity of professional skills, the extensive experience and the commitment to the highest standards of delivery, which they embody.
