Our Story

Family Cake Company specialising in bespoke occasion cakes, corporate cakes, patisserie & divinely indulgent sweet treats.

We are a Family owned company, formerly known as Amato Patisserie, originating in the heart of Soho, and responsible for launching Patisserie Valerie onto the streets of London. After showcasing our creative flare on the cobbles of Old Compton Street we soon evolved into the Family Cake Brand, moving to the hustle and bustle of Kings Cross and working in partnership with:

  • Sands Catering
  • Sandwich man

Our Experience

Our past customers include corporate and small businesses, media companies, and loyal customers throughout Central London and the City.

Our cakes have even made it on the telly, featuring in ITVBe’s The Only Way Is Essex.

All our cakes are made to order by our highly skilled bakers using only the finest quality ingredients.
