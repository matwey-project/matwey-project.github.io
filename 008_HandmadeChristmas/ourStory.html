Our Story

About the Handmade Christmas Co.

Founded in 2012, The Handmade Christmas Co. was established to offer a modern alternative to the traditional Christmas stocking. Five years later, and after reaching more than 100,000 customers around the world, The Handmade Christmas Co. is now the go-to location for personalised Christmas sacks, stockings, baubles, gift wrap and so much more.

The result of years of development by creative duo Tom Coleman and Louis Porter, The Handmade Christmas Co. produces seasonal goods here in the UK, with all products being hand-finished in a London-based studio.

2014

How it started

The Handmade Christmas Co. was founded in 2012 by creative duo, Tom Coleman & Louis Porter, to design a premium alternative to the traditional Christmas stocking.

Following two years of development, the company publicly launched in 2014 with a single personalised Christmas sack design, and a plan to shake up the industry once and for all.

2015

Going online

We worked hard throughout this year, building our core team and working with each of our manufacturers to ensure we had a product of the highest quality – a product we were proud to call our own.

In 2015, a new brand image and website was introduced, welcomed by the launch of the ‘Great British Heritage‘ collection – expanding the sack range to include eight additional designs, each named after distinguished Englishmen from the Elizabethan period.

2016

Expanding our business

2016 saw the launch of the ‘Festively British’ collection, with the introduction of an additional five new sack designs across the personalised and non-personalised collections.

It also marked the start of our high-street expansion, launching partnerships with British retailers, John Lewis and Debenhams. With a selection of our products stocked in every store location as well as online, plus two pop up locations in John Lewis (Oxford Street) and Peter Jones (Sloane Square) offering ‘while you wait’ sack personalisation from September through till December.

2017

Becoming ambitious

2017 has been the most ambitious year yet for the brand. Behind the scenes, we were excited to relocate to our new, 3000 square foot premises in South London – larger than our previous studio. We also invested further into our international expansion, opening an office in Orlando, Florida (USA) to better serve our US customers.

To complement our new physical location, we also launched our new website to greatly improve our customer experience, assist in upgrades to our back-end systems, and to cater for the introduction of our new product ranges – personalised baubles and personalised gift wrap.

2018

With some exciting plans already in place for 2018, we’re looking forward to sharing them with you soon.

We’re always on the lookout for talented individuals to join our ever-increasing team, brands to grow exciting relationships with, and suppliers to develop new products.
