What We Do

Despite significant economic growth, pockets of extreme intergenerational poverty and inequity remain across Australia. Decades of extensive government and non-government programs and services have failed to overcome Australia’s greatest humanitarian crisis.

A Radical Approach

We know change is possible when community leads the way. We are creating a different future for the next generation of First Nations children.

The Children’s Ground model has been developed to influence national reform

It is not one-size-fits all approach. It is not a quick fix.

Our 25-year approach focuses on key areas that effect wellbeing. We aim to evidence what can be achieved when the system is designed by the people it serves. It will be used to advocate for national reform and an Aboriginal-led education system in Australia

How are we different?

We work across 5 intersecting platforms: learning, health, economic wellbeing, culture and community.

Each of these sectors influence wellbeing and Children’s Ground empowers communities across each.

We do this by promoting Aboriginal knowledge systems alongside international best practices.

This combined approach will shift the trajectory for the future for these communities.
