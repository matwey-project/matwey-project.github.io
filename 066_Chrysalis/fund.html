The Board

Andrew Haining

Chairperson –Independent

Andrew has had a 30 year career in banking and private equity with Bank of America, CDC (now Bridgepoint) and Botts & Company. During his career, Andrew has been responsible for over 20 private equity investments with transactional values in excess of $1bn.

Andrew holds several Guernsey and UK board positions including Chairman of Praxis Group Limited and Chairman of Aurigny, the state owned Channel Islands airline.
