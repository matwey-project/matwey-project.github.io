ABOUT US

Based in Australia, with its headquarters in Singapore, H2Coat has built a solid reputation over the years, as a one- stop shop for top-notch products in the marble and tile industry. Led by business leaders in this industry, H2Coat provides superior quality products, coupled with outstanding customer service. Our in-depth perspective regarding the needs of discerning clients is evident in the painstaking research we undertake to bring you products like our marble backing adhesive and temporary floor protection.

Here is why H2Coat’s innovative product line stands apart from the rest of its competition, and distributed across many industries worldwide:

THE TRUE AUSTRALIAN WAY

H2Coat a trusted expert when it comes to specialty products like our temporary surface protection systems and premium performance stone backing adhesive. Getting to the edge of technological advancements in marble and tile, while providing clients with stellar services, is our key motivation.

VISION FOR THE FUTURE

The H2Coat vision for the coming years is to increase profit margins for our stakeholders, and attain key performance indicators in our quest for continuous development. Building our product line and expanding to new markets around the world is one of the significant drivers of our overall brand strategy.

SPECIALISED EXPERTISE COMBINES MODERN RESEARCH

This is the primary reason behind our continued growth in many markets, and our ability to offer a growing selection of products. Highly skilled industry professionals back each unique product we provide to our customers and researchers that strive to raise the bar on innovation in building technologies. For instance, H2Coat has developed a new stone backing adhesive that is designed to increase production line speed with its accelerated drying time, that also creates a strong barrier against damage from salts or moisture.

QUALITY MANAGEMENT AND SEAMLESS PRODUCTION

Modern testing and improvement of mechanical properties and parameters of H2Coat products makes it possible to maintain the highest levels of professionalism. Our team is passionate about pursuing technological innovation in alignment with international methodologies for production processes.

MAKING A GREEN DIFFERENCE WORLDWIDE

Through the years, H2Coat has become synonymous with building extremely specialised product lines that are adapted to industries not just limited to marble and tile. Derek and Ronald, our founders, have a lifetime of experience in the stone industry and this is what remains at the forefront of the H2Coat product line. They remain keen on establishing H2Coat as a recognized brand that identifies modern demands, while preserving traditional values like integrity and transparency.
