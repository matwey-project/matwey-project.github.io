EXPERT BACKING MATERIALS FOR TILE AND STONE

New technology innovations have led to the development of improved protection for tile and stone, using top of the line backing materials. One of our more popular products is the stone backing adhesive MMA-731, which offers a unique single part adhesive that does not require removal prior to fixing. This is a dependable and industry-level quality adhesive for permanently binding fiberglass mesh to natural stone. As a backing material, it was especially designed to greatly increase production line speeds with accelerated drying times, but also not go brittle, peel-off or yellow over time unlike most Epoxy systems.
