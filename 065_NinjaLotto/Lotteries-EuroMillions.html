UK’s Biggest Winners

Since the cap was increased, there has yet to be a €200 million EuroMillions winner. But the previous jackpot cap of €190 million was won four times. Twice in the UK…

£170 Million

Most recently, the €190 million was collected anonymously in October 2019 after a record 22 rollovers with no winner.

The €190 million was equivalent to £170.2 million.

Indeed, the amount won in sterling depends upon the strength of the £ against the € at the time.

£148 Million

The first UK winners of the €190 million were Adrian and Gillian Bayford from Suffolk. They ‘only’ received £148.6 million in August 2012.

Their monster win came after 14 rollovers with no winner.
