We pre-purchase batches of official Lottery Tickets for Lottery Draws, including but not limited to, the UK Lotto, EuroMillions and US PowerBall.

The Company will maintain a record of Syndicate entries for each draw and will calculate the Winnings and distribute them accordingly to each Member of that Syndicate.

The Company takes all reasonable measures to ensure that each individual Syndicate has different Lottery Numbers from other Syndicates, but this cannot be guaranteed, and we accept no responsibility whatsoever for a perceived dilution of winnings in this event.

As the Syndicate organiser, the Company incorporates a fee for managing the Syndicate in the Entry Fee for each Lottery Draw.

Details of cut off times for each individual Lottery Draw are detailed on the Site. Any entry received after the time set out on the Site for the next draw will be included into the following Lottery Draw.

The Company offers different tiers of Syndicate, each with a different number of Lottery Lines purchased for individual Lottery Draws, including but not limited to, Bronze (15 lines), Silver (30 lines), Gold (60 lines) and Diamond (120 lines).

Each Syndicate consists of up to sixty-five (65) Members. Whenever a Syndicate wins a prize, the monies are divided by 65 and distributed equally according to each Members’ share of that Syndicate. For the avoidance of doubt, the distribution is not necessarily based upon the number of people in each winning Syndicate, but instead upon the number of shares (65).

Each Syndicate entry is independent of any other and Members are allocated a unique Syndicate ID Number for each individual draw.
