Personal Information

Personal information means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. We may collect, use, store and transfer the following personal data about you:

Identity: Title, full-name, date or birth, gender, address

Contact Details: Address, email address, telephone numbers

Profile Details: Your username and password

Financial Details: Bank account and payment card details

Transactional Details: Payments to and from you, plus games played and products purchased

Technical Details: IP address, login data, browser type and version, location

Communication Details: Your preferences in receiving marketing from us

We may use your personal information to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information. You can ask us to stop sending you marketing messages at any time by following the opt-out links on any marketing message sent to you.

We do not collect any details about your race or ethnicity, religious or philosophical beliefs, sexual orientation or political opinions.

By law we have to keep basic information about our customers (including Identity, Contact, Financial and Transaction Data) for 5 years after they cease being customers for money laundering purposes.

Cookies

Like most websites, we use cookies to recognise you when your return to our website and to personalise and enhance the service you receive.

Cookies are files with small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some sections of our website.

Third-Party Links

This website includes links to third-party websites (for example Facebook, Twitter etc). In addition, we use third-party services such as Google Analytics that collect, monitor and analyse user activity. We do not control these third-party websites and are not responsible for their privacy statements.

Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures and procedures to prevent the loss or unauthorised access of your personal data. Access to your personal information is restricted to those employees or third parties who have a requirement to know. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee it’s absolute security. In the case of any suspected personal data breach, we will notify you as legally required.

Your Rights

Data protection legislation gives you the following rights over how your personal information is processed.

  • Access to your personal data held by us.
  • Correction of any inaccurate data we hold about you.
  • Erasure of your personal data (subject to legal and regulatory restrictions).
  • Restriction of how we process your personal information.
  • The transfer of your personal information to you or a third party you have chosen.
  • The right to object to the processing of your personal data.
  • The right to withdraw consent to process your personal data.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact dpo@ninjalotto.com. Please note that any erasure or transfer of data, restriction of service or withdrawal of consent means we cannot provide you our products or services.

There is no fee to access your personal data or exercise any of the other rights detailed above, however we may charge a reasonable fee if your request is repetitive or excessive, or even refuse to comply in those circumstances.
