About Us

Ninja Lotto is an independent lottery syndicate service that buys official lottery tickets to enable our customers to play some of the world’s best lotteries alongside other Ninja Lotto players.

Sharing tickets in a managed Ninja Lotto syndicate increases your chances of a big lottery win and saves you money, with the cost per line split with the other players in your syndicate.

Why Ninja?

Ninja were the secret agents of ancient Japan renowned for their stealth, combat skills and tactics to carry out missions.

Contrary to portrayals in modern popular culture, Ninja often operated in groups with teamwork and cooperation essential to achieving community goals.

In order to scale a wall a group of ninja may carry each other on their backs or provide a human platform to assist an individual in reaching greater heights

At Ninja Lotto we adopt the same strategy, joining players together to improve all their chances of a big lottery win.

Simple & Secure

We create syndicates for every UK Lotto, EuroMillions and US PowerBall lottery draw - you simply choose which lottery, how many lines to share and which draw to enter.

It’s so easy to play, with everything already set up for every draw and your lines displayed in your account before each draw you enter.

When one of our syndicates wins a prize, the money is divided amongst the players in that syndicate.

Zero Commissions or Commitments

We operate without taking any commissions from what our users win, no matter how big their prize.

While you can play draws in advance, or subscribe to pay every draw, there is no requirement to enter for more than the next draw - simply enter a syndicate whenever you fancy it.

Official Lotteries

Ninja Lotto is not affiliated with any lottery organisation. However, we purchase all our tickets directly from official lotteries, so playing with us still contributes 100% to their charitable causes.
