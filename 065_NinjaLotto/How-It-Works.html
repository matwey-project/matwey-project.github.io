How It Works

A lottery syndicate is a group of players who buy multiple lottery tickets together to increase all of their chances of winning. Each player receives a proportionate share of all winnings.

What We Do

Ninja Lotto creates syndicates for the world’s greatest lotteries.

We purchase official lottery tickets for every UK Lotto, EuroMillions and US PowerBall draw so you can play alongside other Ninja Lotto players.

We offer syndicates for every draw of each lottery - so you can play whenever you want.

It’s Cheap

Buying into a lottery draw via our syndicates is an affordable way to play multiple lines, as the cost per line is split with the other players in that syndicate.

Pay from as little as 20p per line!

It’s Simple

We make it easy for our players with everything automatically set up for every draw - and whenever one of our syndicates wins a prize, we divide the winnings amongst the players in that syndicate for that draw.

We notify you if your syndicate has won a prize and pay you your share, commission free.

What You Do

Choose from UK Lotto, EuroMillions or US PowerBall.

Play 15 (Bronze), 30 (Silver) or 60 (Gold) lines - the more you play, the cheaper the cost per line.

For example, if you play via a Gold syndicate you will have a share of 60 lines, with 60 chances of winning the jackpot.

Decide how many draws to play - or subscribe to play every draw.

Consult our FAQ’s to find out more about our site and syndicates.
