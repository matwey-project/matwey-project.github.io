WORK FLOW

1Analysis

The first thing to do for the team is to study the app’s concept: how it helps the users to solve their problems, how it helps the business to reach its goals and so on.

Cooperating with the business team, Virtual Electronics finds out what the main challenges are. We also take into account the audience itself. This all results in a project development plan.

2UI/UX

Customer experience means a lot. This is the stage when we also have to evaluate users’ expectations. The goal is to understand how the design of the app can help the business to achieve its goals.

3Solution development

Virtual Electronics uses Agile/Scrum to come up with an initial version of an application, then we gradually improve it add the features that seem to be appropriate. Every stage of this process also includes testing that helps to find out what we need to improve.

4Solution release and further support

Virtual Electronics releases the apps and launches then in the app stores. After that, our teams also provide support.
